Khet: Laser Strategy Game

Desktop Controls

  • Mouse Click: Select and move Silver piece
  • Arrow Keys: Rotate selected Silver piece
  • Spacebar / Tab: Fire laser

Mobile Controls

  • Tap: Select and move Silver piece
  • Swipe Right / Left: Rotate selected Silver piece
  • Swipe Up / Down: Fire laser
KhetAI will respond after you move or rotate a piece.
Press Enter to trigger KhetAI manually.
